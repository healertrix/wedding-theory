# WEDDING THEORY CMS STRUCTURE

## 1. HOMEPAGE CONTENT
* Hero Section
  - Carousel Images
    > Image URL
    

* Introduction Section
  - Welcome Text (Rich text)

* Statistics Display
  - Wedding Count (500+ Weddings Captured)
  - Experience (10+ Years)
  - Cities Coverage (50+ Cities)
  - Client Count (500+ Happy Couples)

* Content Display Rules
  - Wedding Stories: Automatically displays latest 6 stories from Weddings section
  - Blog Posts: Automatically displays latest 6 posts from Blog section

* Video Background Section
  - YouTube Video URL
  - Start Time

* LAHZA Premium Section
  - Cover Image URL
  - Title

## 2. WEDDINGS
* Story Collection
